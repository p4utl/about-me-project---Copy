Work History

Six Flags


I worked at Six Flags Great America in Gurnee, IL as a food service worker during the summer of Sophmore and Junior year of highschool.

Cherry Hill Programs


I worked for Cherry Hill Programs in Gurnee, IL, a photography company that partnered with Simon Malls during the Christmas and Easter seasons. Yes, I was the Easter bunny and it was hot in the bunny suit 😅

Amazon


I currently work at Amazon. I started in 2014 as a In-House Temporary Associate at MKE5 (Sort Center) in Kenosha, WI. In 2015, MKE1 (AR Sortable) opened nextdoor where I was a ICQA Problem Solver and Counter. In 2017, I was promoted to an ICQA Data Analyst at SMF1 (AR Sortable) in Sacramento, CA. In 2019, I helped launch LAS7 (AR Sortable) in Las Vegas, NV. About a year later, I was approached to help launch LAS1 (IXD) in Henderson, NV. Within 6 months, I was promoted to ICQA Program Developer. Presently, I am working out of LAS1, but my IXD+ Central Developer Team is scattered across the country. I build queries and Quicksights for directors and underperforming buildings and regions across the IXD+ Network.
